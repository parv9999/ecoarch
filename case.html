PARKROYAL SINGAPORE EXAMPLE

  • Extensive Greenery:
    • The building has over 15,000 square meters of greenery, equivalent to its total land area.
    • Features tiered "sky gardens" on every fourth floor.
  • Sustainable Design:
    • Uses rainwater harvesting and an automatic irrigation system for its plants.
    • The greenery provides natural insulation, reducing energy costs for air conditioning.
  • Environmental Impact:
    • The lush vegetation reduces the building's heat absorption, lowering its overall temperature.
    • Creates a cooler microclimate in the urban area.
  • Aesthetic and Social Space:
    • The design blurs the line between indoor and outdoor spaces, offering a unique experience for guests.
    • The gardens are accessible, providing a tranquil space in the city.
